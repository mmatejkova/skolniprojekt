Feeding and Monitoring Seagulls in Sydney

Join us on Sunday, December 10th, from 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM, at Bondi Beach in Sydney for a unique opportunity to care for and observe local seagull populations. As scavengers, seagulls often face dangers from ingesting plastic and other waste, but with your help, we can support healthier habits.

Volunteers will distribute safe, nutrient-rich food while recording seagull behavior and numbers to help inform future conservation efforts. You’ll also help clean feeding areas and ensure that waste is disposed of properly. This activity is perfect for bird enthusiasts and those eager to learn more about coastal ecosystems!

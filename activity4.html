Coral Reef Restoration in the Maldives

Dive into the world of marine conservation by participating in a coral reef restoration project in the Maldives on Monday, January 22nd, from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Coral reefs are the lifeblood of ocean ecosystems, but they are under threat from bleaching, pollution, and climate change.

Volunteers will assist in planting coral fragments, removing invasive species, and monitoring reef health. Whether you’re an experienced diver or new to underwater activities, training will be provided. Together, we’ll work to rebuild these vibrant ecosystems, giving marine life a safe and thriving home.

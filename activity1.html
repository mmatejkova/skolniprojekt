Beach Plastic Cleanup at Crystal Cove

Get ready to make a real impact! Join us on Saturday, December 9th, from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, for a plastic cleanup event at beautiful Crystal Cove State Beach. This is your chance to directly contribute to protecting marine life by removing harmful waste that pollutes the shore and threatens ocean ecosystems.

We'll provide all the supplies you need, including gloves, bags, and guidance to ensure an efficient and safe cleanup. Come as you are—solo, with friends, or bring the family—and enjoy a meaningful day by the sea. Along the way, you’ll learn about the dangers of ocean pollution and how your efforts contribute to the health of marine animals and habitats.

Let’s work together to make Crystal Cove a cleaner, safer place for all creatures who call it home. Your time and energy can truly make a difference!

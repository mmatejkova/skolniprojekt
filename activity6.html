Supporting Traditional Fishing in Zanzibar

Immerse yourself in the culture of Zanzibar by supporting traditional fishing communities on Thursday, January 4th, from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Small-scale fishers play a vital role in sustainable marine management, but they often face challenges due to overfishing and habitat destruction.

As a volunteer, you’ll help repair fishing nets, clean up the shores, and participate in workshops to promote sustainable fishing practices. You’ll also get the chance to learn firsthand about traditional fishing techniques and the importance of preserving these practices for future generations. Together, we can empower local communities while protecting the ocean’s resources!

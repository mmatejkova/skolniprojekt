Sea Turtle Nest Patrol in Costa Rica

Protect sea turtles during nesting season by joining our patrol team on Friday, December 15th, from 7:00 PM to 1:00 AM, along the beaches of Costa Rica. Turtles face numerous threats, including poaching, predators, and habitat disruption, and your help can make all the difference.

As a volunteer, you’ll monitor nesting sites, record turtle activity, and assist in relocating vulnerable eggs to safe hatcheries. No prior experience is needed—just a love for marine life and a desire to help! This unforgettable experience will bring you closer to these ancient creatures while making a real impact on their survival.

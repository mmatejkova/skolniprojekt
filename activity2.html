Penguin Habitat Restoration in Patagonia

Join us in Patagonia on Saturday, January 13th, from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, for a meaningful day of restoring penguin nesting habitats! Penguins rely on safe, undisturbed spaces to lay their eggs and raise their chicks, but pollution, erosion, and human activity have degraded these areas.

Volunteers will help clear debris, plant native vegetation, and build barriers to protect nests from predators and disturbances. You’ll also have the chance to observe penguins in their natural environment and learn about the unique challenges they face due to climate change and overfishing. Together, we can help these charismatic birds thrive!

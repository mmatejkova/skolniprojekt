Volunteer

Dive into action and make a tangible difference for marine life! Our free volunteer programs allow you to play an active role in protecting the oceans. From cleaning up plastic on beaches to rescuing injured turtles and restoring vital habitats, there are countless ways to contribute. Volunteering is a chance to connect with like-minded individuals, learn about ocean conservation, and see the direct impact of your efforts. Whether you have a few hours or a whole weekend, your time can help create a healthier, thriving marine ecosystem.

Join us and be the change our oceans need!
